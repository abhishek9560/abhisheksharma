How it works

  • Insert/Tap Card – ATM reads your card chip or stripe.
  • Enter PIN – You verify your identity.
  • Local Check – ATM processes card & PIN, sends request to bank.
  • Bank Request – ATM sends encrypted data to issuer via switch (NPCI/Visa/Master).
  • Issuer Verification – Bank checks PIN, balance, and card status.
  • Response – Bank approves or declines the transaction.
  • Cash Out – ATM dispenses cash and prints receipt.
  • Settlement – Later, banks settle the amount and fees.
